# SeasonXI Scene Blocks
# Append one of these after the season mood block.
# Final structure: base + player + season + scene
#
# Not all scenes need a generated image.
# Hook = must generate (main player image)
# Card Reveal = reuse Hook image
# OVR/Graph/Stats = UI-only (no image generation needed)
# Commentary = optional background image
# Achievement = optional background image
# Verdict = UI-only


## HOOK
Scene context:
- this is the opening reveal shot
- player should dominate the entire frame
- full body or strong 3/4 body
- facing slightly toward camera
- maximum presence and aura
- strongest lighting and atmosphere
- space at the bottom 20% for text overlay
- the image should feel like the player is "appearing" or "emerging"
- highest drama and intensity of all scenes


## HOOK_CLOSEUP
Scene context:
- tight upper body or face-focused composition
- intense eye contact toward camera
- dramatic rim lighting on face and shoulders
- space at bottom 30% for text overlay
- this is for when you want a more intimate, intense hook
- maximum emotional weight


## EMOTION_CLOSEUP
Scene context:
- this is a mid-video emotional shot, NOT the opening
- tight face and upper chest framing
- the player should look focused, intense, or in the zone
- sweat on skin, rain particles, or breath visible for realism
- dramatic side lighting creating strong shadows on face
- dark moody background, very shallow depth of field
- the feeling should be "this player was unstoppable"
- space at bottom 25% for one line of text overlay
- more intimate and emotional than the hook shot
- cinematic portrait feel, like a movie close-up


## CARD_BACKGROUND
Scene context:
- this is a background-only image, no player figure
- dark stadium atmosphere from behind the goal
- cinematic floodlights creating god rays
- wet pitch reflecting light
- subtle fog or mist
- empty but atmospheric
- designed to sit behind a card UI overlay
- very dark, mostly silhouette and light


## COMMENTARY_BG
Scene context:
- atmospheric background for a text-heavy scene
- abstract football motion blur
- diagonal light streaks suggesting speed
- very dark with subtle golden light accents
- no player visible
- no ball visible
- pure mood and atmosphere
- text will be placed over this image
- keep it minimal and cinematic


## ACHIEVEMENT_BG
Scene context:
- background for a big number / achievement display
- stadium interior shot from pitch level
- looking up at floodlights
- dramatic upward perspective
- subtle lens flare
- goal net visible but soft-focused
- celebratory atmosphere without visible players
- dark with golden highlight accents
- space in center for large text/number overlay


## DUAL_POSE
Scene context:
- same player shown in TWO different poses side by side
- left side: controlled, composed stance
- right side: explosive, dynamic action
- split composition with subtle dividing line
- this is for comparison or before/after scenes
- both poses should feel like the same player in the same session
- maintain consistent lighting across both sides
