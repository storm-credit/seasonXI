## MODIFIERS — 생성 결과 미세 조정용
## 필요할 때만 프롬프트 끝에 추가

## TOP_TEXT_SAFE_SPACE
Leave clean empty space in the upper 20% of the image for title text overlay.

## CLEANER_SILHOUETTE
Use a cleaner composition with a more readable silhouette and less background distraction.

## STRONG_AURA
Add a stronger mythic aura burst behind the player while keeping the scene elegant.

## SIMPLE_KIT
Keep the kit simple, realistic, premium, and readable. Avoid overdesigned fantasy details.

## RECOGNIZABLE_FACE_VIBE
Make the player clearly recognizable by facial vibe, hairstyle, beard, and body language, but not an exact copy.

## LESS_BACKGROUND
Reduce background complexity. Focus on the player with a simpler, darker backdrop.

## MORE_DRAMATIC
Increase cinematic drama. Stronger light beams, more particles, deeper contrast.

## CARD_FRAME_FIT
Ensure the player fits cleanly within a vertical card frame. Leave margin on all sides for cropping.

## LOWER_BODY_VISIBLE
Make sure the full body including feet and ball are visible. Do not crop below the knees.

## CALM_EXPRESSION
The player should have a calm, focused, confident expression rather than aggressive or shouting.
